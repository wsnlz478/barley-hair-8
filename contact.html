Commonly Asked Questions

Everything you ought to understand about getting in touch and scheduling your hair transplant in China

What's the most effective way for an overseas patient to get in touch with Barley?
For the fastest reply, we suggest messaging us on WhatsApp at +86 13730143529. WhatsApp serves as our main communication channel for international patients, and our overseas coordinators usually reply within 2-4 hours during working hours. WeChat (ID: 15810155700) and email (hairtransplant@qq.com) are equally excellent alternatives.
Is there English-speaking personnel available?
Absolutely. Our international patient division is operated entirely by bilingual professionals fluent in both English and Mandarin. From your opening message to your post-operative check-ups, all communication happens in English. Numerous surgeons on our team have also received international training and can discuss your treatment plan in English throughout consultations.
How soon can I expect a response after making contact?
We strive to answer every inquiry within 2-4 hours during our operating schedule (9:00 AM to 8:00 PM China Standard Time, seven days per week). Messages received outside these windows receive priority attention the following morning. For the quickest turnaround, WhatsApp remains your ideal choice.
What's the process for scheduling a complimentary hair transplant consultation?
It couldn't be simpler — just send us a note via WhatsApp, WeChat, or email including your name, a short summary of your hair loss situation, and your preferred consultation date. Our international coordinator will validate your booking within 24 hours and forward all necessary details, including preparation steps and what to anticipate.
Is it possible to have an online consultation before flying to China?
Without a doubt. We actually recommend that all overseas patients kick things off with a virtual consultation. During your online session, our physician will evaluate your hair condition through photographs or a live video call, explore treatment possibilities, estimate the graft count required, and provide an initial cost range. This enables you to reach a well-informed decision before arranging any travel.
What materials should I have ready ahead of my consultation?
For the most precise evaluation, please prepare high-quality photographs of your scalp — front, crown, back, and both sides. If you possess earlier photos showing your hair from previous years, those prove extremely valuable for tracking your hair loss progression. It's also beneficial to compile a list of any medications you're currently using, previous treatments you've attempted, and any health conditions potentially relevant to surgery.
Is the consultation genuinely free without any hidden obligations?
Indeed — entirely complimentary and completely free of any commitment. Our consultations exist to help you explore your possibilities, regardless of whether you ultimately select Barley or a different provider. We will never push you toward booking a procedure. Our sole objective is to deliver candid, expert guidance so you can arrive at the right choice for your situation.
Do you assist with logistics for travelers coming from abroad?
We certainly do. Our overseas patient services encompass airport collection, hotel reservations close to the clinic, and assistance with ground transportation. Numerous partner hotels provide reduced rates exclusively for our patients. We can also issue an official invitation letter should you require one for your visa submission. Our aim is to make your complete journey as effortless as possible.
What does the reservation procedure involve?
Following your consultation, should you choose to proceed, securing your appointment is simple: (1) We validate your procedure date, graft quantity, and total fee. (2) A deposit is needed to lock in your slot — we'll forward payment details aligned with your preferred method. (3) Once everything is finalized, we'll dispatch a thorough preparation guide outlining what to do in the days leading up to surgery and what to bring along for your journey.
How early should I make my reservation?
We advise reserving your spot a minimum of 4-6 weeks ahead of your intended travel date, particularly during busy periods (March through May and September through November). This provides sufficient time to organize transportation, obtain a visa if necessary, and get ready for the procedure. That being said, we can occasionally work with tighter timelines — just reach out and we'll make every effort to accommodate you.
Which payment options are available for patients from overseas?
We welcome international wire transfers, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and WeChat Pay. For more extensive procedures, we're glad to explore installment plan possibilities. The complete procedure fee is generally expected on the day of surgery. We'll guide you through all pricing and payment specifics during your consultation so you encounter no unexpected charges.
Is a visa required to visit China for a hair transplant?
Depending on your citizenship, a visa may be necessary. Most visitors need either a tourist visa (L visa) or a medical visa to enter China. We can supply an official invitation letter from our medical facility to bolster your application. We encourage you to verify the visa prerequisites for your country and initiate the process no later than 4 weeks prior to your intended departure.
How do time zones impact communication?
China follows China Standard Time (CST, UTC+8). This places us 12-16 hours ahead of the Americas, 7-8 hours ahead of Europe, and 1-2 hours ahead of Southeast Asia. Our operating window runs from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM CST. Even if you reach out outside these hours, we'll respond promptly the following business day. We're highly conscious of time zone differences and work hard to remain accessible.
Am I able to travel with a companion?
Without question — a large number of our international patients arrive accompanied by a spouse, partner, or close friend. We're more than happy to look after your companion throughout the entire experience. They're invited to attend your consultation, participate in pre-operative discussions, and remain by your side throughout recovery. We can also assist with finding appropriate lodging for both of you.
What type of post-operative support is available after I return home?
Following your procedure, you'll be provided with detailed aftercare guidelines in English. We arrange follow-up consultations at 1 week, 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, and 12 months post-surgery. Our international patient liaisons remain reachable through WhatsApp and email for any questions throughout your healing period. We additionally provide photo-based progress evaluations enabling our surgeons to track your growth from a distance. You'll never feel unsupported during your recovery.
What happens if I need to cancel or change my appointment?
We understand that itineraries shift. Should you need to rearrange your date, simply notify us at least 7 days prior to your appointment and we'll happily identify an alternative that suits your schedule. Your deposit carries over fully to your rescheduled session. Should you need to withdraw completely, our cancellation terms will be transparently outlined during booking — no concealed charges, no unpleasant surprises.
