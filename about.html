Barley Clinic Overview

Comprehensive answers to your questions about Barley Hair Transplant Hospital

How long has Barley been performing hair transplants?
Barley was founded in 2006, making us one of the earliest adopters of modern hair transplant technology in China. Over 18+ years, we've performed more than 100,000 procedures and continuously refined our techniques. Our longevity in this field means we've worked through every type of hair loss case, treated patients from over 50 countries, and developed the expertise that comes only from sustained, focused practice.
Are Barley's surgeons board-certified?
Yes. Every surgeon at Barley is board-certified in either plastic surgery or dermatology before joining our team. Beyond that baseline, they complete an additional 12-18 months of specialized hair transplant training at our facility, during which they observe and assist in hundreds of procedures before performing their first independent surgery. Ongoing education is mandatory, with quarterly peer reviews and annual participation in international conferences.
What are Sullivan certifications and why do they matter?
Sullivan certifications are internationally recognized quality benchmarks that evaluate clinical outcomes, patient safety, facility standards, and operational performance through independent auditing. Barley holds 8 Sullivan certifications across multiple domains including microneedle transplantation excellence and international patient services. These certifications matter because they're verified by third-party auditors, not self-reported. They confirm that our claims about quality and safety are independently validated.
Can I verify Barley's patents independently?
Absolutely. All 14 of our patents are registered with the China National Intellectual Property Administration (CNIPA) and can be searched in their public database. We encourage patients to conduct their own due diligence. During your consultation, we're happy to provide patent numbers and documentation. These patents cover our microneedle implant pen, graft preservation systems, extraction tools, and density optimization algorithms.
How many clinics does Barley operate and are they all the same quality?
Barley operates 30+ clinics across major Chinese cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Chengdu, and others. Critically, all clinics are directly owned and operated by Barley — there are no franchises or licensed locations. This means every clinic follows identical protocols, uses the same patented equipment, and employs surgeons trained to the same standards. We conduct quarterly unannounced quality audits at every location to ensure consistency.
What is Barley's graft survival rate?
Our graft survival rate exceeds 95%, which is significantly higher than the global industry average of 85-92%. This superior performance results from our microneedle technology, temperature-controlled graft storage at 4-8°C throughout the procedure, minimal handling time, and the surgical experience gained from over 100,000 procedures. We track survival rates meticulously and are transparent about these outcomes during consultations.
Has Barley treated international patients before?
Yes, we have extensive experience with international patients from over 50 countries. Our international patient program includes dedicated English-speaking coordinators, visa assistance, airport pickup, hotel arrangements, and comprehensive remote follow-up care. We understand the unique concerns of traveling abroad for surgery and have refined our processes to make the experience as seamless as possible for overseas visitors.
Do Barley's surgeons speak English?
Many of our surgeons speak fluent English and can conduct consultations and post-operative discussions directly in English. Regardless, you'll have a dedicated English-speaking coordinator throughout your journey who translates medical discussions and ensures clear communication. All consent forms, medical records, and aftercare instructions are provided in professional English.
What is Barley's patient satisfaction rate?
Our patient satisfaction rate exceeds 98%, based on post-procedure surveys and follow-up feedback. This high satisfaction reflects not just surgical outcomes, but the entire patient experience — from initial consultation through long-term follow-up. We take patient feedback seriously and continuously refine our processes based on what patients tell us matters most to them.
Are Barley's surgeons recognized internationally?
Yes. Our surgeons have been invited to present at the ISHRS (International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery) World Congress multiple times, which is the most prestigious international gathering for hair transplant professionals. They've published peer-reviewed research on microneedle transplantation outcomes and several hold positions on international hair restoration educational committees. This recognition confirms that our work meets global standards of excellence.
How does Barley ensure quality control across so many locations?
We use a centralized quality management system. All surgeons complete the same intensive training program. Equipment and supplies are distributed from our central network. We conduct unannounced quarterly audits at every clinic. Patient outcomes are tracked across all locations and reviewed by our medical advisory board. This systematic approach ensures that whether you're treated in Shanghai or Xi'an, the quality standards are identical.
Can I see before-and-after photos of actual Barley patients?
Yes. Our Cases page features an extensive collection of genuine before-and-after photographs organized by procedure type. All photos are taken under standardized lighting with no digital alteration. During your consultation, we can show you cases similar to your specific hair loss pattern so you have realistic expectations. We also encourage you to request patient references if you'd like to hear directly from former international patients.
What makes Barley different from other Chinese hair transplant clinics?
Three key factors: First, we developed our own microneedle technology and hold 14 patents — we're not using someone else's equipment. Second, we've been doing this since 2006 with 100,000+ procedures — our experience is unmatched. Third, we maintain 8 Sullivan certifications verified by independent auditors. Many clinics claim quality; we provide documented, independently verified proof.
What is Barley's mission and philosophy?
Our mission is to restore confidence through natural, permanent hair restoration. We believe in treating the whole person, not just the scalp. Our philosophy centers on six principles: patient-centered care, continuous innovation, uncompromising quality, complete transparency, collaborative teamwork, and international patient focus. These aren't marketing slogans — they guide how we train staff, structure procedures, and interact with every patient.
How can I verify Barley's credentials before booking?
We encourage independent verification. Our patents can be searched in the CNIPA database. Sullivan certifications can be confirmed through the Sullivan organization. We're happy to provide clinic operating licenses, surgeon board certifications, and credential documentation upon request. We believe transparency builds trust, and we welcome patients who do their homework before committing.
