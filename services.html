Common Questions About Our Procedures

Discover responses to the most frequently asked questions from our clientele

Which hair transplant service is right for me?
The right service depends on your specific hair loss pattern, donor hair availability, and aesthetic goals. During your free consultation, our surgeons will assess your scalp, discuss your expectations, and recommend the best approach — whether that's hairline restoration, crown thickening, eyebrow transplant, or a combination. We'll be honest about what's achievable and what's not.
Can I combine multiple procedures in one session?
Yes, many patients combine procedures. For example, you might do hairline restoration and crown thickening in the same session, or add a beard transplant to your scalp procedure. Combining treatments saves time and can be more cost-effective. Your surgeon will advise on safe graft limits per session and whether staging treatments across multiple visits would produce better results.
How many grafts do I need for my procedure?
Graft count varies by procedure and individual needs. Hairline restoration typically requires 1,000-2,000 grafts. Crown thickening may need 1,500-3,000 grafts. Extensive baldness restoration can require 3,000-4,500+ grafts, sometimes staged across two sessions. During your consultation, we'll calculate the exact number needed based on your hair loss pattern, donor density, and desired results.
What's the difference between hairline transplant and hair thickening?
Hairline transplant focuses on restoring the frontal hairline — creating a natural, age-appropriate frame for your face. Hair thickening (also called density enhancement) addresses diffuse thinning across the top or crown of the scalp by adding grafts between existing hairs to increase overall volume. Many patients benefit from both procedures, either combined or staged.
Is the no-shave option available for all procedures?
The no-shave option is available for most procedures, particularly hairline restoration and hair thickening. It's ideal for patients who need discretion and can't take time off work. However, for very large sessions requiring maximum grafts, shaving may be necessary for optimal visibility and efficiency. Your surgeon will discuss whether no-shave is suitable for your specific case during consultation.
How long does each procedure take?
Procedure time depends on graft count. A typical session of 2,000-3,000 grafts takes 6-8 hours. Smaller procedures like eyebrow or beard transplants may take 3-5 hours. Extensive restoration requiring 4,000+ grafts might be split across two days. You'll receive a detailed timeline during your consultation based on your specific treatment plan.
What's the recovery time for different procedures?
Recovery is similar across all procedures with our microneedle technology. Most patients return to office work in 3-5 days. You can wash your hair gently after 24 hours. Avoid strenuous exercise for 10-14 days. The tiny incisions heal quickly with minimal scarring. Eyebrow and beard transplants follow the same recovery timeline as scalp procedures.
Do you treat female hair loss?
Yes, we treat many international female patients. Female hair loss often presents differently than male pattern baldness — typically as diffuse thinning rather than receding hairline. Our surgeons use specialized techniques for women, including gentle placement between existing hairs and no-shave options for discretion. We also offer non-surgical treatments like PRP therapy for early-stage thinning.
What is PRP therapy and when is it recommended?
PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy uses growth factors from your own blood to stimulate hair follicles, strengthen existing hair, and slow hair loss. It's recommended for early-stage thinning, as a complement to transplant surgery, or for patients who aren't candidates for surgery. Sessions are quick (30-45 minutes) and typically performed monthly for 3-6 months, then maintained quarterly.
Can I see examples of your work before committing?
Absolutely. Our Cases page shows genuine before-and-after photos organized by procedure type. During your consultation, we'll show you cases similar to your specific hair loss pattern so you have realistic expectations. We can also connect you with former international patients willing to share their experiences if you'd like firsthand feedback.
What if I need a second session?
For extensive hair loss, we sometimes recommend staging treatment across two sessions spaced 3-6 months apart. This allows us to maximize results while ensuring safe graft limits and manageable recovery. If you need a touch-up after your initial procedure, we provide complimentary minor refinements within the first year. For international patients, we assess touch-up needs via video consultation first.
How much does each service cost?
Pricing varies by procedure and graft count. Hairline restoration typically ranges $2,000-3,500 USD. Hair thickening runs $2,500-4,500 USD. Extensive baldness restoration can be $3,500-6,000 USD. Eyebrow and beard transplants are usually $1,500-2,500 USD. Our prices are 50-70% lower than US/UK/Australia for equivalent or superior quality. You'll receive a detailed quote during your free consultation.
Are non-surgical treatments effective?
Non-surgical treatments like PRP therapy, medications (finasteride, minoxidil), and scalp care programs work best for early-stage thinning and can slow hair loss progression. They're less effective for advanced baldness where follicles are already gone. During consultation, we'll honestly assess whether non-surgical options will meet your goals or if transplant would be more appropriate.
What should I avoid before my procedure?
Stop blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen), certain supplements (fish oil, vitamin E), alcohol, and smoking 1-2 weeks before surgery. These affect bleeding, healing, and graft survival. We'll provide a detailed pre-procedure checklist. International patients should arrive in China at least 1-2 days before their scheduled procedure to settle in and complete pre-op assessments.
When will I see results after my procedure?
Transplanted hair sheds within 2-4 weeks (this is normal). New growth begins around month 3-4. You'll see significant improvement by month 6, with final results at 9-12 months as hair thickens and matures. The waiting requires patience, but results are permanent — these follicles come from the genetically stable donor area and will continue growing for life.
