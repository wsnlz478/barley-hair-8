Common Questions About Our Surgeons

Key details regarding our medical staff and their professional backgrounds

How can I verify that Barley's surgeons are properly licensed and qualified?
All Barley surgeons hold active medical licenses issued by China's National Health Commission, and many have additional board certifications in plastic surgery or dermatology. During your consultation, you can request to see credentials, and we provide documentation of professional memberships in organizations like the Asian Medical Hair Restoration Association. We understand that international patients need reassurance, so we make verification straightforward and transparent.
What is the average experience level of surgeons performing my procedure?
Our lead surgeons have each completed over 5,000 hair transplant procedures individually, with several senior doctors surpassing the 10,000-case mark. Across our entire medical team, Barley has performed more than 100,000 successful procedures since 2006. This depth of experience means your surgeon has likely encountered and successfully managed cases very similar to yours.
Can I request a specific surgeon for my procedure?
Absolutely. You're welcome to review our complete physician directory and request any doctor whose expertise aligns with your needs. During your initial consultation, our coordinator will also recommend the specialist best suited to your specific situation—whether that involves hairline design, corrective revision, or female pattern restoration. Your comfort and confidence in your surgeon are our priority.
Will the same surgeon who consults me also perform the procedure?
Yes. The doctor who examines your scalp, designs your hairline, and determines your graft count will personally carry out your surgery. This continuity ensures that the agreed-upon plan is executed exactly as discussed. That same physician also manages your post-operative examination and all follow-up visits, so you're never handed off to someone unfamiliar with your case.
Do certain surgeons specialize in specific techniques or patient types?
Yes. While every Barley surgeon is proficient across the full spectrum of transplant methods, individual practitioners have distinct areas of strength. Some excel in artistic hairline creation, others focus on covering extensive baldness, repairing failed prior transplants, or treating female diffuse thinning. We match each patient with the specialist whose expertise best fits their unique situation.
What training do new Barley surgeons complete before operating independently?
Our onboarding follows a rigorous, phased structure. New surgeons begin with intensive classroom instruction on our proprietary techniques, then assist on hundreds of cases under senior mentorship, then operate with direct supervision before earning clearance for independent practice. This apprenticeship typically spans 12 to 18 months before a surgeon receives full certification in our system.
Who actually performs the extraction and implantation during surgery?
The lead surgeon directs every phase and personally handles the most critical stages—including hairline design, recipient site formation, and placement of grafts in the most visible frontal zone. A trained surgical team assists with follicle extraction and graft preparation under the surgeon's close supervision. This collaborative model optimizes efficiency without sacrificing quality or safety.
How much experience do your doctors have with international patients?
Our clinical team routinely treats individuals from North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, Australia, and the Middle East. Our physicians understand the varied hair textures, aesthetic ideals, and cultural nuances that come with different ethnic backgrounds. This breadth of international exposure ensures your outcome will appear natural within the context of your own heritage and personal style.
Can I review before-and-after photos from a specific surgeon?
During your consultation, we present a curated portfolio of cases completed by any physician you're considering. We maintain thorough records organized by surgeon, procedure type, and hair loss classification. You'll have the opportunity to examine outcomes from cases closely resembling your own circumstances before making a decision.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the results?
We take patient satisfaction very seriously. If your results fall short of mutually agreed expectations, the operating surgeon will re-evaluate your case and explore corrective options. In the rare event that graft survival drops below our guaranteed minimum, we provide complimentary touch-up procedures. Every stage of your surgery is documented for complete transparency and accountability.
Do Barley surgeons offer non-surgical hair loss treatments as well?
Yes. Our doctors are qualified to assess and prescribe across the full range of hair loss therapies, including PRP injections, low-level laser therapy, pharmaceutical options like finasteride and minoxidil, and targeted nutritional supplements. Many patients achieve optimal long-term density by combining surgical intervention with ongoing medical maintenance.
How do Barley surgeons stay current with advances in hair restoration?
Our physicians regularly attend global conferences such as the ISHRS World Congress and regional medical hair restoration symposia. They contribute to peer-reviewed research, exchange insights with international colleagues, and continually refine our clinical protocols. Our in-house research and development team also creates new instruments and techniques grounded in the latest scientific evidence.
What is the surgeon-to-patient ratio during the procedure?
Every procedure is staffed with one lead surgeon, one or two assisting physicians, and a team of trained technicians. For a standard 2,500-graft session, a minimum of five to six medical professionals will be devoted exclusively to your case. This arrangement allows extraction, graft preparation, and implantation to proceed in parallel, minimizing the time follicles spend outside the body.
Can I have a video consultation with my surgeon before traveling?
Certainly. We offer video consultations via WhatsApp, WeChat, or Zoom before your trip. During this session, your designated surgeon will examine photographs of your scalp, discuss your goals, outline the proposed strategy, and address any concerns. Establishing this connection beforehand gives you confidence and peace of mind well before you board your flight.
Does the surgeon stay involved throughout my recovery?
Your surgeon personally examines your scalp before you leave the clinic and schedules follow-up assessments at one week, one month, three months, six months, and twelve months. For international patients, these check-ins take place through photo uploads and video calls. The same doctor who performed your surgery monitors your progress at every milestone, ensuring continuity of care.
