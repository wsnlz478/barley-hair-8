Visa & Entry: Step-by-Step Guide

The Surgery: What to Expect

Understanding your procedure options and what happens on surgery day

Type Description Duration Recovery Best For
FUE Minimally invasive, short recovery, leaves small scars 4-8 hrs 7 days Small/medium bald areas; short hairstyles
FUT Suitable for large-scale transplants, longer recovery, linear scar 2-4 hrs 14 days Large-scale restoration; low donor density
No-Shave Undetectable procedure without shaving donor area 6-10 hrs 5 days Professionals who can't take visible downtime

International Patient Questions Answered

Common questions from international patients about hair transplant in China

Do I need a visa to enter China for hair transplant?
Citizens of 54 countries (including USA, Canada, Australia, UK, and most EU nations) can use the 240-hour transit visa-free policy. Citizens of certain countries like France and Singapore enjoy 30-day visa-free entry. For longer stays, you'll need a tourist (L) visa or medical (S2/S1) visa. Barley provides invitation letters to support your visa application.
How much can I save by choosing China over Western countries?
You can save 50-70% compared to US, UK, or European prices. A 3,000-graft FUE procedure costs $4,000-$7,500 in China (all-inclusive), versus $15,000-$25,000 in the US or $10,000-$18,000 in the UK. Even after adding flights and accommodation, you'll save thousands of dollars with no compromise on quality.
How long do I need to stay in China for the procedure?
The minimum stay is 7-10 days: Day 1 for arrival and pre-op tests, Day 2 for surgery, Days 3-7 for initial recovery, Day 8 for follow-up and suture removal (FUT only), Day 9-10 for departure. We recommend adding 2-3 buffer days for flexibility.
Do you provide English-speaking staff?
Yes, Barley has dedicated English-speaking coordinators who assist you throughout your journey — from initial consultation to post-op follow-up. Our surgeons also speak English and can explain procedures in detail. We provide 24/7 WhatsApp support in English.
What if I have complications after returning home?
Barley provides remote follow-up support via WhatsApp and video calls. We can coordinate with local doctors in your country if needed. Our 95%+ graft survival rate and comprehensive aftercare instructions minimize complications. We also provide emergency contact numbers for urgent situations.
Can I see before-and-after photos of international patients?
Yes, we have extensive before-and-after documentation of international patients from 50+ countries. During your virtual consultation, we'll show you cases similar to your hair loss pattern. You can also view our Cases page for sample results.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards (3% processing fee), bank transfers (requires 5-day advance notice), and cash payments under $5,000. We provide detailed invoices for insurance or reimbursement purposes. A deposit secures your surgery date, with the balance due upon arrival.
Is the quality of care the same as Western countries?
Yes, Barley meets or exceeds international standards. Our surgeons are members of CAPA and ISHRS, we hold 14 technology patents, and maintain 95%+ graft survival rates. Our facilities feature sterile operating rooms, advanced equipment, and comprehensive safety protocols. Many of our techniques are more advanced than those used in Western clinics.
